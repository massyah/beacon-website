Software Engineer (Backend)

Job Description

DNAstack is looking for an experienced software engineer to join an energetic and growing team of scientists and engineers focused on delivering an easy-to-use platform to store, query, and visualize very large (petabyte) datasets of genetic variants. While the primary responsibilities for this position comprise the development of robust and secure software, implementing new features and functionality, and evolving our existing architecture, you will also be involved in client meetings, developing specifications, and conducting demonstrations.

Required Experience

  • Undergraduate or graduate (preferred) degree in computer science, computer engineering, or bioinformatics.
  • 3+ years of software development experience in Java (preferred).
  • Familiarity with principles of software engineering, and common design patterns.
  • Experienced with SQL, and configuring and running a DBMS (MySQL preferred) for development.
  • Proficiency using Git for version control.

Desired Experience

  • Experienced writing parallelized, cloud-based software that operates on very large datasets, using technologies including Google BigQuery, App Engine, and Apache Hadoop.
  • Proficient with AngularJS, JavaScript, and HTML5, to assist our UI/UX designers in delivering a fast and responsive user experience.
  • Knowledge in any of the following areas would be considered an asset for this position: statistics, R, machine learning, human genetics or genomics, computational biology or bioinformatics, preparation of patent applications, mobile application development (Android or IOS)
  • In addition, this position may occasionally require working with tools written in languages such as Perl, Python, or C/C++.
